About the Project

Meet AG-Bot

AG-Bot is our team's entry for the RoboTech Competition — an autonomous agricultural robot designed to tackle real-world farming challenges with precision and intelligence.

Built from the ground up, AG-Bot combines seed planting mechanics, pH soil measurement, and environmental sensing into a compact, efficient system that can navigate fields autonomously.

Our goal: make smart agriculture accessible, reduce manual labor, and help farmers make data-driven decisions about their soil health.

Autonomous Navigation Seed Planting pH Analysis Soil Sensing Eco-Friendly
What AG-Bot Can Do

Core Features

01
Seed Planting

AG-Bot precisely plants seeds at optimal depth and spacing using its mechanical arm system, ensuring maximum germination rates.

02
pH Measurement

Real-time soil pH analysis with high-precision sensors. Get instant readings to understand your soil's acidity and nutrient availability.

03
Soil Analysis

Multi-parameter soil sensing monitors moisture, temperature, and composition to provide comprehensive land health reports.

04
Autonomous Navigation

AG-Bot navigates fields independently using onboard sensors and path-planning algorithms, covering maximum area efficiently.

05
Data Logging

All sensor readings are recorded and stored for trend analysis. Track your soil health over time and make informed farming decisions.

06
Eco-Efficient Design

Designed with sustainability in mind — low energy consumption, minimal soil disruption, and zero chemical usage in operation.

The Process

How AG-Bot Works

01
Field Deployment

AG-Bot is placed at the starting point of the agricultural field. The operator sets the target zone and planting parameters through the control interface, and the robot calibrates its sensors for the environment.

02
Soil Scanning

Before planting, AG-Bot scans each section of the field. Its pH sensor and moisture probes analyze the soil composition in real-time, building a map of the land's nutritional state.

03
Precision Planting

Based on soil data, AG-Bot activates its seed planting mechanism, placing seeds at the precise depth and spacing needed for optimal growth. It adjusts dynamically based on real-time readings.

04
Data Reporting

All data collected during the operation is compiled into a full field report — pH levels, moisture content, planting coordinates, and recommendations for soil improvement.
